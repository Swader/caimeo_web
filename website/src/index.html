The Future of Offline & On-Chain AI

Caimeo lets you develop, run, and verify AI agents in a purely local environment-or optionally connect to TEE and on-chain solutions for tamper-proof results.

Framework Core

Local-First Determinism

Run closed-loop simulations with verifiable random seeds. Perfect for research, prototyping, or security-sensitive AI tasks.

Advanced Plugin System

Customize environments with hazards, grid-based challenges, or TEE-based verification for guaranteed tamper-proof outcomes.

Open Standards

Compatible with multiple LLMs and built on established protocols like EVM RMRK for optional NFT expansions and modular AI traits.

On-Chain Integration (Optional)

Composable Agents

Agents become NFTs with equippable modules. Ideal for “agent games,” competitive AI, or scenarios requiring public trust in results.

Verifiable Results

Use Phala TEE or upcoming ZK proofs to confirm simulation integrity on-chain, bridging offline experimentation with decentralized transparency.

Rewards & Tokenomics

Set up token-based incentives for agent performance or environment creation. The official experiment: Caimeo Agents.

Use Cases

Some ways developers, researchers, and creators are exploring Caimeo:

Market Simulations

Model complex trading or consumer behavior with local AI agents. Then publish results or partial proofs on-chain for transparency.

Crisis Response Training

Run advanced scenarios (fires, intrusions, disasters) to test AI strategies in deterministic steps. Reward or share top outcomes publicly.

Game AI & NPCs

Develop emergent in-game agents that can evolve or adapt. Optionally anchor them as NFTs to prove their authentic performance or skill set.

Education & Research

Build AI tutors or research simulations without network dependencies, then share proofs or partial data to communities when relevant.

About Caimeo Agents

Evolution System

Soul Shards progress into Agents with new abilities. Each level adds “slots” for behaviors or personality traits. This process is a proof-of-concept showing how the Caimeo framework handles progressive state changes over time.

Social Performance

Agents can level up via community actions, governance participation, or scenario achievements.

Market Performance

AI that engage in trading or market models can earn specialized skill modules or “win points.”

Intelligence Growth

Plug in new LLM-based behaviors, personality traits, or domain knowledge, forging unique digital identities.

Bridge Guide: Solana ‚Üí Polygon

Use the Wormhole Portal Bridge to move your CAIMEO from Solana to Polygon. The process takes approximately 15-20 minutes.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Go to Portal Bridge
    Open portalbridge.com/legacy-tools/#/transfer
  2. Connect Your Solana Wallet
    Click "Connect" and select your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
  3. Select Source & Target
    • Source Chain: Solana
    • Target Chain: Polygon
  4. Enter Token Address
    Paste the Solana CAIMEO mint address: 61Kav5B7mWLum8xcnrCefdrUsysQhpB7KdNTKiMApump
  5. Enter Amount
    Input the amount of CAIMEO you want to bridge
  6. Connect Polygon Wallet
    Connect your MetaMask or other EVM wallet for Polygon
  7. Confirm & Sign
    Review the transaction and sign with your Solana wallet
  8. Wait for Confirmation
    The bridge typically takes 15-20 minutes. Keep the page open.
  9. Redeem on Polygon
    Once confirmed, click "Redeem" to receive your wrapped CAIMEO (wCAIMEO) on Polygon

Swap wCAIMEO ‚Üí Native CAIMEO

After bridging, you'll receive wrapped CAIMEO (wCAIMEO) on Polygon. To get native CAIMEO, you need to swap 1:1 using our swap contract.

Why Swap?

  • Native CAIMEO has cleaner metadata and branding
  • Native CAIMEO supports ERC-3009 for gasless payments (x402 compatible)
  • Native CAIMEO is the token used in Uniswap liquidity pools

How to Swap (Manual Method)

  1. Approve the Swap Contract
    Go to the wCAIMEO contract on Polygonscan and approve the native CAIMEO contract to spend your tokens:
    • Spender: 0x86001E1A161eE746A0A2506060d8Fff9C58dd24c
    • Amount: Your wCAIMEO balance (or max uint256 for unlimited)
  2. Call the Swap Function
    Go to the native CAIMEO contract, connect your wallet, and call swap(amount) with your wCAIMEO amount (in 6-decimal format).

Tip: 1 CAIMEO = 1,000,000 in 6-decimal format. So 100 CAIMEO = 100000000.
